Maple Cinnamon Cake

Follow this for someone's life story.

Descrption

A glueten free option for a soft and delicious bundt cake.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups blanched almond flour
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 3 Tbs Ground Cinnamon
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3/4 Cup Full-Fat Coconut Milk
  • 1 Cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a bundt cake pan with coconut oil (VERY important!)
  2. In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, coconut milk, syrup, and vanilla.
  4. Stir into dry ingredients and pour into greased pan.
  5. Bake in oven for 30-40 minutes, until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let cool for 30 minutes (VERY important!) on a wire rack before turning upside-down onto a serving platter. Let cool another 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
